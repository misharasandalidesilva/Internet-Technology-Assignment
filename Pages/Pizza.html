Pizza Recipe

Pizza, a beloved dish originating from Naples, Italy, consists of a round or rectangular dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings. It evolved from ancient flatbreads and has become a global favorite, celebrated for its versatility and endless variations. From the classic Margherita to creative specialty pizzas, it brings people together and offers a delicious canvas for culinary creativity.

Chicken Pizza

Ingredients of Chicken Pizza

For the Dough
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1 ½ cups warm water (110°F/45°C)
  • 3 ½ to 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
For the Sauce
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
For the Chicken Topping
  • 1 cup cooked chicken, shredded or diced (grilled, baked, or rotisserie chicken works well)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
For the Pizza
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Optional: Sliced bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, or other preferred vegetables

Instructions

1. Prepare the Dough
  • Activate the Yeast: In a small bowl, dissolve the sugar in the warm water. Sprinkle the yeast over the top and let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  • Mix the Dough: In a large bowl, combine 3 ½ cups of flour and salt. Make a well in the center and add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir until a dough starts to form.
  • Knead the Dough: Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes until it’s smooth and elastic. Add more flour if needed to keep it from sticking.
  • Let the Dough Rise: Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours or until it has doubled in size.
2. Prepare the Chicken
  • Season the Chicken: In a bowl, toss the cooked chicken with olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  • Set Aside Set the seasoned chicken aside to use later.
3. Prepare the Sauce
  • In a small saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  • Add the tomato sauce, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Stir and let it simmer for about 10 minutes. Adjust seasoning as needed.
4. Preheat the Oven
  • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while it preheats.
5. Shape the Dough
  • Punch down the risen dough and transfer it to a floured surface. Roll or stretch it out into your desired shape (circle or rectangle) to fit your baking sheet or pizza stone. If using a baking sheet, lightly oil it before placing the dough on it.
6. Assemble the Pizza
  • Spread the Sauce: Evenly spread a layer of pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  • Add Cheese: Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  • Add Chicken and Toppings: Distribute the seasoned chicken evenly over the cheese. Add optional vegetables if desired. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top.
7. Bake the Pizza
  • Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned. If using a pizza stone, bake directly on the stone for a crisper crust.
8. Cool and Serve
  • Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. This helps the cheese set a bit and makes it easier to cut.





Pepperoni Pizza

Ingredients of Pepperoni Pizza

For the Dough
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast (1 packet)
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (110°F/45°C)
  • 3 1/2 to 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
For the Sauce
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, for heat)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
For the Topping
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup sliced pepperoni
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional, for garnish)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1. Prepare the Dough
  • Activate the Yeast: In a small bowl, dissolve the sugar in the warm water. Sprinkle the yeast over the top and let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  • Mix the Dough: In a large bowl, combine 3 ½ cups of flour and salt. Make a well in the center and add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir until a dough starts to form.
  • Knead the Dough: Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes until it’s smooth and elastic. Add more flour if needed to keep it from sticking.
  • Let it Rise: Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a clean cloth or plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-1.5 hours, or until it has doubled in size.
3. Prepare the Sauce
  • Combine ingrediants: In a medium bowl, mix together the tomato sauce, tomato paste, olive oil, oregano, basil, garlic powder, and red pepper flakes (if using).
  • season: Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper as needed.
4. Preheat the Oven
  • Set Temperature: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while it preheats.
5. Shape the Dough
  • Punch Down: Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release air bubbles.
  • Roll Out: Turn the dough onto a floured surface and roll it out to your desired thickness (about 1/4 inch thick is typical). If using a pizza stone, transfer the rolled dough to a pizza peel or an inverted baking sheet dusted with flour.
6. Assemble the Pizza
  • Add Sauce: Spread a thin layer of the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  • Add Cheese: Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  • Add Pepperoni: Arrange the sliced pepperoni evenly on top of the cheese.
7. Bake the Pizza
  • Bake: Transfer the pizza to the preheated oven (or onto the pizza stone if using one). Bake for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
8. Finish and Serve
  • Cool: Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
  • Garnish If desired, garnish with fresh basil leaves.
  • Slice and Enjoy Slice the pizza and serve it hot!
